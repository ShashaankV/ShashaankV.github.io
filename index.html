I made a smartphone tool for this and working with mobile EEGs to optimize this system. I was fortunate to be a speaker on this at IPAM, NIMH, and SIAM LS. (Thank you to my colleagues.) Goal: cittatech.org as landing page for past and future work for these tools (in-progress)

I did a bit of work on genomic analysis methods at the NIH (part of Plink2, extended by major labs and the startup Genomic Prediction).

I was fortunate to work with great teams on Bayesian COVID pandemic modeling, and Bayesian Medicare claims analyses.
